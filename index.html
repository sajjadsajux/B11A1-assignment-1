The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions progress through Digital Transformation, creating a nationwide digital ecosystem for connectivity and accessible services. Green Energy investments in solar and wind ensure a sustainable future. Modern Infrastructure development supports growth and global competitiveness.

Date: 05/01/2025   Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh has successfully launched its new satellite, enhancing communication, weather forecasting, and disaster management capabilities. Bangladesh Unveils Solar Power Plant

Date: 07/01/2025   Time: 11.15 PM

Bangladesh has launched its largest solar power plant, aiming to boost renewable energy production and reduce dependency on fossil fuels. The plant is expected to provide clean energy to millions, help meet national energy goals, and contribute to the country's efforts to combat climate change.

Date: 05/01/2025   Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh has opened a new tech innovation hub designed to support startups and entrepreneurs. The hub will offer funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities to foster the growth of local businesses and stimulate the nation’s digital economy, paving the way for future innovations.
